Frequently Asked Questions

Skill Hive is a peer-to-peer platform where users can teach skills they have and learn new skills from others through live, one-on-one video sessions.

You can add, edit, or remove your skills by navigating to your Profile page and clicking the "Edit Profile" button. You can list the skills you want to teach in the skills input field.

All sessions are conducted via a secure, in-browser video call. Once a session is scheduled and confirmed, both users will receive a link to join the live session page at the agreed-upon time.

Skill Hive is free to join. The platform operates on a credit or time-based system, which is currently in development. Basic features like searching and profile creation are free.
